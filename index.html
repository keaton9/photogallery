About me

Hi! My name is Keaton Powell.

I am an junior at Virginia Tech majoringing in Public Relations with a minor in Marketing Management. I just got married. I just moved to a small town, and I just opened up an antique shop. I might be just like you, but except from the time I was a little girl I knew I could talk to the dead. Earthbound spirits my grandmother called them. The ones who have not crossed over becasue they have unfinished business with the living and they come to me for help. In order to tell you my story, I have to tell you theirs.
